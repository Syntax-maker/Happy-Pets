About Us

We Keep Your Pets Happy All Time

Come and participate in the experience with us and make ‘HAPPY PETS’ your one-stop look for all your dog’s needs. All things considered, it is the adoration they merit.

Our mission in the pet industry is to create a pet food shop that will be known to offer some of the very best and quality pet products and services at affordable costs to customers.

We are starting our pet food shop small, but that does not mean our vision for Happy Pet Store, is small. We intend to grow big immediately after startup, and our vision is to be known as the leading pet food shop in Metro Manila, and within a few years
